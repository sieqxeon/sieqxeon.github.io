App Information

VoiceCount

VoiceCount is a simple application that counts spoken words in real time using voice recognition. It can be used for speech practice, presentations, language learning, or hands-free counting.

  • Features
    • Real-time voice recognition
    • Counts spoken words automatically
    • Hands-free operation
    • Simple and lightweight interface

Privacy Policy

This application does not collect personally identifiable information.

This app may use third-party services such as Google AdMob that may collect information used to identify you.

The information collected by these third parties is used for providing and improving their services.
